STATEMENT OF FACTS
On May 22, 2013, the Plaintiff, Mary Ann Johnson, was carefully and prudently stopped in her motor vehicle on Route 152, when her vehicle was violently struck from behind by the Defendant.
At the same time, a motor vehicle operated by James C. Sexton, Defendant, was traveling, and smashed into the Plaintiffâ€™s vehicle, causing a car accident in which the Plaintiff sustained severe personal injuries.
